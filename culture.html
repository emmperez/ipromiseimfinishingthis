Life at IMPULSE

At IMPLUSE, work-life balance isn’t just a concept—it’s a way of life. Flexible hours and remote work options ensure you can unleash your creativity on your own terms. Here, we don’t just think outside the box; we turn the box into a masterpiece, and then build a fort out of it. Welcome to a place where work feels like play, and every day is an opportunity to create something amazing.

The Power of Not Being the Same

Within our close-knit team, we treasure the diverse backgrounds, cultures, and perspectives each person brings. Our members form a vibrant tapestry of experiences, talents, and skills, enriching our collective creativity.

We are a design agency based in New York City. With a strong belief in strategically founded and highly crafted digital experiences, we strive to create remarkable solutions that captivate our clients and their audiences.

Compact Team

Small but Agile

The heart of our culture beats with collaboration and playfulness. Mornings often kick off with impromptu jam sessions in the music corner, while afternoons might see a friendly showdown at the ping-pong table. We celebrate quirky theme days, from Pajama Mondays to Superhero Fridays, adding a dash of fun to our routine. Our snack bar is legendary, offering everything from gourmet popcorn to artisanal sodas, keeping energy levels high and spirits even higher.

One of the defining aspects of our agency is our optimal team size. We have discovered that maintaining a compact team of between 15 and 20 members allows us to achieve the best results for our clients and ourselves. By deliberately keeping our team size within this range, we ensure efficient collaboration, open communication, and seamless coordination.
